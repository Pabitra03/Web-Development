The Toyota Supra is a legendary sports car known for its performance, tuning potential, and distinct design. Originally introduced in 1978 as a variant of the Celica, the Supra evolved into a standalone model, gaining fame through its A80 (1993-2002) and A90 (2019-present) generations.

The Nissan GT-R is a high-performance sports car famed for its advanced engineering, powerful drivetrain, and cutting-edge technology. First introduced in 1969 under the Skyline GT-R nameplate, the car gained legendary status, especially with the R32-R34 generations (1989-2002).
Creator:- Shinichiro Sakurai
The Honda Civic Type R is a high-performance version of the popular Civic. First introduced in 1997, the Type R took the Civic platform to new heights, incorporating advanced performance upgrades, including lightweight materials, a tuned suspension, and high-revving engines.
